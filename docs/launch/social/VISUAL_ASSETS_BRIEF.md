## 🖼️ LOGO

### Primary Logo Description

```text
A modern, geometric fusion symbol combining:

1. ATOMIC ORBITAL
   - Two or three elliptical paths crossing at center
   - Represents quantum computing / electron orbitals
   - Subtle gradient along paths (purple to blue)

2. NEURAL NODE (Optional)
   - Small circles at intersections of orbitals
   - Represents AI / neural networks
   - Connected by thin lines

3. CORE
   - Central circle or sphere
   - Solid or gradient fill (purple to cyan)
   - Represents the fusion of paradigms

STYLE:
- Clean, minimalist lines
- No heavy gradients (subtle only)
- Works at 16x16 favicon to 4000x4000 print
- Single colour versions available (white, black)

AVOID:
- Overly complex details
- 3D effects that don't scale
- Trendy effects that will date
```text

### Logo Variations Needed

1. **Full Logo**: Icon + "Fusion" wordmark
2. **Icon Only**: Square format for avatars
3. **Favicon**: 16x16, 32x32, 48x48
4. **Monochrome**: White on dark, black on light
5. **Animated**: Subtle rotation for video intros

### Logo Sizes

| Use           | Size                | Format                |
| :------------ | :------------------ | :-------------------- |
| Favicon       | 16x16, 32x32, 48x48 | ICO, PNG              |
| Social Avatar | 400x400             | PNG                   |
| Social Banner | 1500x500 (Twitter)  | PNG                   |
| Print         | 4000x4000           | SVG, PNG              |
| Light bg      | Various             | PNG with transparency |
| Dark bg       | Various             | PNG with transparency |
