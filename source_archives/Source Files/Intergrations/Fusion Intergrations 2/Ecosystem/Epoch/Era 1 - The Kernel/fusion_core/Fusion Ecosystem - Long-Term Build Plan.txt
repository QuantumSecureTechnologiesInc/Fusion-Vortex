Fusion Ecosystem: Long-Term Build Plan
Document Version: 1.0
Status: Strategic Roadmap
Scope: 15 Domains (~10,500 Crates)

🗺️ Executive Summary
The Fusion Ecosystem build is divided into two distinct eras. The "5-Phase Plan" currently visible in the definitive list is Era 1 (The Kernel). This document outlines Era 2 (The Ecosystem), detailing how the 10,000+ crates across AI, Quantum, and Security domains are layered on top of that kernel.

🏗️ Era 1: The Kernel (Phases 1-5)
Goal: Build the language, compiler, and type system.
Target: v0.2.0 Release (The "Bootstrapping" Phase)
Phase 1-3: fusion_core (Classical, Tensor, Quantum types).
Phase 4: Compiler Semantic Analysis (Hybrid type checking).
Phase 5: Compiler Backend (LLVM/WASM codegen) and Validation.
Outcome: We have a working compiler that understands Matrix, Qubit, and int. We have zero high-level libraries (no HTTP, no Neural Networks, no K8s).

🌍 Era 2: The Ecosystem Expansion
Goal: Build the 15 Domains on top of the Kernel.
Strategy: Parallel execution across four "Epochs".

Epoch 1: The Foundation (v0.3.0 - v0.5.0)
Focus: Standard Library, Tooling, and Interop.
Without these, developers cannot write useful code.

Epoch 3: The Specialized Pillars (v0.9.0)
Focus: The massive specialized domains. These are built in parallel by specialized teams.
Pillar A: Artificial Intelligence
Domain VI (AI/ML): fusion_tensor_core_fp8, fusion_dl_transformer, fusion_graph_nn.
Domain XI (Governance): fusion_ai_bias_detector.
Dependency: Requires stable Tensor Types from Era 1.
Pillar B: Quantum Computing
Domain VII (Quantum): fusion_q_compiler (Full optimization), Hardware Bridges (IBM/IonQ).
Dependency: Requires stable Quantum Types from Era 1.
Pillar C: High-Performance Systems
Domain XIV (Finance): fusion_hft_engine, fusion_fix_connector.
Domain XV (Geospatial): fusion_gis_engine, fusion_digital_twin.
Dependency: Requires Zero-GC paths in Runtime.
Epoch 4: The Enterprise Platform (v1.0.0+)
Focus: Compliance, Verification, and Large-Scale Orchestration.
