Product Guide: Fusion ForgeComponent ID: F-FORGE-001Classification: Build SystemAuthor: DevTooling Team1. Vision & PurposeFusion Forge is our hermetic build orchestrator. It is designed to replace cargo build and make by ensuring that every build occurs in a clean, isolated environment where external factors (environment variables, system paths) cannot pollute the output.2. Key FeaturesWasm Sandboxing: Build scripts cannot access your file system or network unless explicitly granted permission in the manifest.Zero-Rebuild Logic: Forge hashes the content of files, not the timestamp. If you change a comment, Forge knows the AST is identical and skips the re-compile.4. Why this existsStandard Rust builds are often non-reproducible due to ambient state on the developer's machine. Forge enforces a "Clean Room" policy for every compilation.
