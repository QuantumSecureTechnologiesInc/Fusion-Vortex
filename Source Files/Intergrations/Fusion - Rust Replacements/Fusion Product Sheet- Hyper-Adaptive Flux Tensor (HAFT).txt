Product Guide: Hyper-Adaptive Flux Tensor (HAFT)Component ID: F-COMPUTE-COREClassification: Data PrimitiveAuthor: AI/Compute Team1. Vision & PurposeHAFT is the native tensor primitive for the Fusion language. It makes differentiable programming a first-class feature of the language, rather than a library add-on.2. Technical Replacement SpecsReplaces: ndarray, PyTorch Tensors, numpy.array.Type Signature: tensor<T, Shape> where Shape is a compile-time constant.3. Key FeaturesAuto-Diff: Automatic differentiation is built into the compiler. You can call .backward() on any scalar produced from a HAFT tensor.Type-Safe Shapes: The compiler prevents you from multiplying a [3, 4] tensor by a [5, 6] tensor at compile time.4. Why this existsTo solve the "Two-Language Problem." In Fusion, your high-level logic and your neural network layers are written in the same language, compiled by the same tool, and optimized together.
